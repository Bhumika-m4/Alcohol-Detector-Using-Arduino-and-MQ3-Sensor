# Aim, Objectives and Methodology

## Aim
To develop an Alcohol Detection System using Arduino UNO and MQ3 Sensor for real-time alcohol monitoring and safety enhancement.

## Objectives
1. Detect alcohol vapors accurately using MQ3 sensor.
2. Provide immediate alerts through LED and buzzer.
3. Improve safety by preventing alcohol-related accidents.
4. Develop a low-cost and easy-to-use embedded system.
5. Gain practical knowledge of sensor interfacing and embedded programming.

## Methodology

The project begins with a detailed study of alcohol sensing technologies and available sensors. The MQ3 sensor was selected due to its high sensitivity toward alcohol vapors and low cost.

The sensor is connected to the Arduino UNO, which continuously reads the analog output generated by the sensor. Sensor calibration is performed to determine a suitable threshold value.

The Arduino processes the sensor readings and compares them with the threshold. When alcohol concentration exceeds the limit, the buzzer and LED are activated. Testing is performed using alcohol-containing substances such as hand sanitizer to validate system performance.

The overall methodology ensures accurate detection, reliable alert generation, and practical implementation.
